TECH – Brand Identity Description

This poster presents a modern and futuristic brand identity for TECH, a concept that blends human intelligence with advanced technology. The logo features a stylized human face combined with a neural network pattern, symbolizing the connection between AI, data, and innovation.

The design uses a vibrant color palette with shades of blue, pink, and purple on a dark background, creating a strong tech-driven and digital feel. These colors represent creativity, connectivity, and forward-thinking innovation.

The typography uses bold and clean fonts like Orbitron and Poppins, giving a professional yet futuristic appearance. The glitch-style effect in the word “TECH” highlights the concept of digital transformation and coding culture.

The app icons and mockups demonstrate how the logo can be applied across different platforms such as mobile apps and merchandise, ensuring versatility and brand consistency.
